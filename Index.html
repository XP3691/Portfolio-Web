3D Modeling Project: Sunken Car


3D Modeling Practice Topology and Lighting

Wireframe View




Step 1: Modeling The Car

In the beginning, this project was all about learning how to properly model vehicles.
After some trial and error, I finally landed on this Dodge Challenger Hellcat, my fourth car model.
To start i picked out a blueprint and set it up in my scene so all the orthographic
views are properly lined up.

Next I started work on a low poly version of the car. After i was done i applied subdivision and smooth modifiers.

Low poly model no smoothing or subdiv.

Model after cleanup subdiv. and smooth are applied.

Lastly I started working on all the car components that had to be sepparately attached,
i aslo turend the single mesh into multiple car panels to give it a more detailed and complex look
(this part also includes all details on the side of the car).

Step 2: Making the scene

After Finishing the Vehicle i wanted to create a proper scene to showcase the model. I am presonally
a huge fan of surreal scenes, especially the ones that include a mundane item. After some trial i
landed on the concept of an underwater scene with the car floating in the middle.

Scene Layout using particle system for seaweed and rocks.

Adding all the scene assets (fish and jellyfish are from BlenderKit).

Step 3: Volumetrics & Lighting

Once Everything was put in place it was time to add lighting and mood to the scene,
as the scene takes place underwater it required caustics for the lighting i achieved
this by creating a transparent plane with a voronoi and displacement map on it.
For the lighting i used a spotlight with extremely high luminosity to simulate
sunlight and to create the foggy water effect i used a volumetric modifier on a texture

Second Year Game Design Project

Assignemnt to Design a game in unity.

Part 1 The Gameplay.

The Assignment consisted of creating a game that showcased our understadning of both
visible and invisible game design components, for this i decided to create a 3d movement
platformer with a look that reminds the player of the PsX era games.

Movement Tech: Wallrunning

The Player is taught to interact very early on, once they reach this part of the sublevel
they wont be able to progress without unlocking the wallrunning ability by ineracting with the
computer in front, in case they try to continue the walls do not have collision and the player
will fall through.



Once Unlocked, The Player Will be able to run on the Yellow colored walls around
the world, this allows the player to explore higher up areas on the map.



Movement Tech: Grappling

The level is designed in a way that they will most likely find the wallrunning sublevel first
and if they do not they will run into a roadblock regardless as they lack the ability to interact
with the yellow walls. Once this is completed however they will most likely have collected at least
50 CD collectibles which allows them to buy the grappling block at the ATM which then allows them to
grapple on purple BLocks



Once Unlocked, The Player Will be able to run on the Yellow colored walls around
the world, this allows the player to explore higher up areas on the map.
